The All-Hands Care Collective

Coordinating free web-based mental health services for California COVID-19 frontline health workers.

As the COVID-19 pandemic intensifies, health workers responding to the crisis may benefit from additional support to help maintain personal health and wellness, work-life-family balance, and a sense of agency and purpose at work. We are here to empower and support you while you care for others.

WHO ARE WE?

A statewide collective of mental health providers responding to the COVID-19 public health crisis.

WHAT DO WE OFFER?

Free, web-based support for frontline health workers including ER and ICU doctors, nurses, and support staff, internal medicine providers, paramedics, etc.

REASONS TO CONNECT

  • To try something new, optimize your work performance, and/or maintain your health and wellness
  • Sadness, depression, and/or apathy
  • Feeling isolated or disconnected
  • Insomnia, fatigue, and exhaustion
  • Anger, irritability, or overwhelm
  • Increased alcohol or drug use
  • Trouble maintaining healthy work-life-family balance
  • Stress and anxiety

HOW DO I CONNECT?

If you are seeking support, fill out the form below and we will connect you with a provider.

If you are volunteering your services, fill out the form below and we will connect you with frontline health workers.
